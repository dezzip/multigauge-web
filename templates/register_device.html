How to find your Hardware ID


The Hardware ID is your ESP32's MAC address. You can find it by:


1. Connecting your ESP32 via USB

2. Opening the Serial Monitor

3. The MAC address is printed on boot


Format: AA:BB:CC:DD:EE:FF
